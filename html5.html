Semantic HTML - Want to label your elements according to their function on the page. Here are some tags:

HTML5 header tag used multiple times on a page. Can include introductory or navigational elements.

HTML5 nav tag holds navigational items. A nav tag can go inside of a header.

HTML5 footer tag holds information about the company or person that produced content on the page.

HTML5 aside tag is for information that does not directly relate to the main content on the page.

HTML5 section tag groups related information together.
